Products.

Multicurrency

Registers all operations in different currencies, (dollars, euros) in multicurrency accounts and automatically calculates exchange rates adjustments and provides multicurrency information.

Financial Management

Be able to manage business funds, in different cash, bank and custody accounts. Check account management. Financial Budgets Cash-flow reports, bank account reconciliation. Cash & expenses Scoreboard and reports with main indicators.

Inventory management & Payables

Registers buys in different currencies managing different warehouses. Product traceability, Budget & invoice input. Management of Credit, Interest and past due calculations. Registers all tax and withholding taxes involved (VAT, Sales Tax, Income Tax) Different payment methods (cash, checks, promissory notes, bank transfers). Calculates costs based on FIFO, LIFO, Issue of payment orders.

Inventory management & Receivables

 

Get organized and get paid. Registers sales of product & services, in different currencies managing different warehouses and controlling stock availability. Product traceability, Budget, manual and automated billing. Credit management & interest and past due calculations. Registers all tax and withholding taxes involved (VAT, Sales Tax, Income Tax) Collections with different payment methods (cash, checks, promissory notes, bank transfers). Credit lines and credit limit management, Issue of Receipts.

Loan origination and Loan servicing

Manages all business loans, (line of credit , secured and unsecured consumer loans , commercial real estate, commercial and industrial, mortgage) allows management of  securitized portfolios. Supports term loans , lines of credits, fixed ,floating and step interest , French, German, American, Revolving and Direct amortization plans, variable, seasonal,ballon and interest only payment structures.automates billing and accountability, calculates fees, commissions & expenses involved. penalty interest Allows to monitor and manage loan collections, create collection strategies,past due & legal. Issue of Loan agreements Automated late payment reminders (via sms, email) Manages all relevant loan documentation, and digitalizes agreements. . (Paperless) Attach PDF or Image documentation.Mobile collection input (Offline -Online) using smart phones.

Mobile Offline collections

Provides management of all collection processes done by your agents through their mobile devices offline & online.
